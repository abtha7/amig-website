About Our Organization

The Aerospace Medicine Interest Group is dedicated to exploring the fascinating intersection of medicine and aviation. We bring together medical students, faculty, and professionals interested in the unique challenges and opportunities in aerospace medicine.

What We Do

  • Host guest speakers and clinical educators
  • Discuss current topics in aerospace medicine
  • Explore career pathways and opportunities
  • Connect students with mentors and research opportunities
  • Build a community of aerospace medicine enthusiasts

Join Us

Whether you're curious about aviation physiology, interested in becoming an aerospace medicine physician, or simply want to learn more about this specialized field, we welcome you! Contact one of our officers to get involved.

Research Opportunities

Interested in conducting research in aerospace medicine? Below are opportunities for students to get involved.

Aviation Medical Examiner Shadowing
Shadow an Aviation Medical Examiner to learn about medical certification of pilots and the practical aspects of aerospace medicine in clinical practice.
Contact for details →
Hypoxia & Altitude Research
Participate in research studying the physiological effects of altitude and hypoxia. Great opportunity to learn about research methodology and respiratory physiology.
Contact for details →
Pilot Health & Telemedicine
Explore innovative approaches to remote medical care for pilots and remote locations. Combines aerospace medicine with modern technology.
Contact for details →
Spatial Disorientation & Vestibular Research
Study the vestibular system and spatial disorientation in aviation contexts. This research has direct clinical applications.
